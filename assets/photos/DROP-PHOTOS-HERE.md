# Drop real photos here

This business's photos are Facebook-only and can't be scraped automatically.

**To finish this site with real images:**
1. Open the business's Facebook page → Photos.
2. Save 6–10 of the best shots into THIS folder (any filename, .jpg or .png).
   - Include 1–2 wide/landscape shots (good for the hero) + a variety for the gallery.
3. Tell Claude "photos are in <business>" and it'll pick the hero, build the gallery, and remove the placeholders.
