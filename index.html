Our Story

Two decades of
Louisiana celebrations.

For more than twenty years, Clark's Cakes & Confections has been the name south Louisiana families turn to for the cake at the center of the table. Owned and operated by cake artist Clark Robertson, the shop is built on a simple pair of promises: special attention to every detail, and true excellence in flavor.

Nothing here is off a shelf. Each order is baked fresh and designed around you — your event, your colors, your idea sketched on a napkin or saved from a magazine. Clark works with you from first concept to final delivery, so the cake that arrives is exactly the one you pictured.

That care is why Clark's has become a word-of-mouth institution around Livingston — and why the calendar fills so far ahead. The 2026 wedding season is fully booked.

What We Make

Cakes for every kind
of getting-together.

From the once-in-a-lifetime centerpiece to the treats that make a Tuesday feel special — all custom, all made to order.

Wedding Cakes

Tiered, timeless, and unmistakably yours — designed at a consultation to match your flowers, palette, and the feel of your day. Our signature.
